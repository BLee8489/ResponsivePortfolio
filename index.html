About Me

Hi there! My name is Bonnie, and I love all dogs/ puppies. I have a Boston Terrier named Mia. I was born in Hayward, buy grew up in Pleasanton almost all of my life. I then moved to Daly City when I went to SFSU for college, since it is on the border between SF and Daly City. I ended up staying there even after I finished, because I was working in the city, and my friends all lived out there too. Fast forward to now, I currently live in Walnut Creek, and I work for a tech based real estate company called Compass. Some of my favorite things to do are trying new foods/ restaurants, traveling around the world, and hanging out with my dog! Coding is hard, but I'm looking forward to getting better at it! Lol.
